About this page

This website is part of my submission to the CodeDex.io Holiday Mini Hackathon!

I’m SynthSloth, and I'm passionate about the World of Web development. This page is just a small part of my journey into learning and exploring web development.


As for the retro theme, it was inspired by my love for the 80s and 90s internet culture. I wanted to bring back that nostalgic feeling—the clunky, pixelated graphics, flashy colors, and the ever-present sense of excitement. These elements remind me of how the internet used to be: fun, quirky, and unpredictable.

This challenge was a perfect opportunity to channel that nostalgia while learning and experimenting with modern web development tools. I hope you enjoy your time here, as much as I enjoyed building it!


Cheers,
SynthSloth – A Web Development Enthusiast
